Our story

ORIGINS

Born from a Seed of Urgency

YBTC was born from a single, powerful realization: India’s ecological and economic future hinges on regenerative action today. Sparked by the urgency of climate change, soil degradation, and rural distress, our founders set out to build a solution that was both scalable and soul-driven. The humble yet mighty bamboo emerged as the perfect vehicle—a fast-growing, carbon-absorbing giant with deep roots in India’s culture and economy. YBTC began with one goal: plant prosperity, grow green futures.

GROWTH

Plant by Plant, Village by Village

From our first pilot plantations to acres of thriving bamboo ecosystems, YBTC has rapidly grown into a movement. We’ve partnered with farmers, inspired corporate changemakers, and welcomed everyday citizens into our mission. Every bamboo plant we grow is not just a carbon sink—it’s a job, a skill, a community uplifted. Alongside plantations, we’ve created Green Labs, Bamboo Parks, and the Oxygen Village experience to engage, educate, and energize people of all ages.

FUTURE

A Million Bamboo Dreams

Our ambition is bold: to establish India’s largest decentralized carbon credit network, powered by bamboo and verified by blockchain. We’re building a future where rural livelihoods thrive, cities breathe easier, and India becomes a global leader in climate-positive action. With technology, trust, and transparency at the core, YBTC is not just planting bamboo—it’s planting belief. Join us as we turn land into legacy, and impact into inheritance.

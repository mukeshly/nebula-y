Why Bamboo is India’s Green Gold

  • Ecological, Social & Economic Superplant

    Bamboo grows fast, captures carbon, and restores degraded land. It creates rural jobs, supports artisans, and boosts local economies.

    • Become a Sponsor
  • Driven by the Government of India

    Under the National Bamboo Mission, India is promoting bamboo as a climate-resilient, income-generating crop — especially for small farmers.

    • Become a Sponsor
    • Download Govt Report
  • A ₹25,000 Crore Opportunity

    India’s bamboo industry is rapidly expanding — across furniture, fiber, fuel, food, and carbon credits. The world wants sustainable materials, and bamboo leads the way.

    • Become a Sponsor
  • More Than Just a Plant

    Bamboo becomes paper, flooring, charcoal, bioplastics, textiles, building material — and even toothbrushes. The by-products are endless, profitable, and planet-friendly.
